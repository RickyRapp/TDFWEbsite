Mission

You do the giving,
we do the everything else.

The Donor's Fund is on a mission to return the autonomy, ease, joy and fulfillment of direct giving.

We empower donors of every size with breakthrough financial tools, granting formats, self-service technologies, and lower fee structures-in addition to all inherent Donor Advised Funds benefits-reconnecting you with what you really care about: charitable causes and decisive donations.

This enables you to be as strategic and entrepreneurial with your giving as you are with your earning. The result is more frequent grantmaking, in more scenarios, to more of your favorite charities, for greater charitable impact.

That's how we set you Free to giveTM.
